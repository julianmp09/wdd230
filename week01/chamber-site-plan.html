Overview

Purpose

My purpose is create the website for a small town called domselaar. The Domselaar Chamber of Commerce acts as a source of information about the town's businesses, increasing the town's growth.

Scenarios

What has been the population growth in the area?

How will this site help more people learn about the businesses in town?

How will it help the town's entrepreneurs attract the attention of consumers?
